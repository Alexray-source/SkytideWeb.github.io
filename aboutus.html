About us!

SkyTide is an amateur game studio with the goal of making games that gamers can enjoy. We are a tiny and a new group and not very experienced at the moment. At the current moment we are a ROBLOX game development group.

This will change in 2021 as SkyTide is aiming to be a standalone studio.
